What Is Lightspeed Squarespace Integration by SKUPlugs?

Lightspeed POS is a robust retail management platform used by retailers to run in-store sales, manage inventory, and track customers. Squarespace is a popular website builder known for its elegant templates and easy-to-use commerce features. When those platforms aren’t connected, stores commonly struggle with duplicate entries, stock mismatches, and manual updates.

SKUPlugs bridges that gap by providing an automated connector that synchronizes products, inventory, pricing, and orders between Lightspeed and Squarespace—reducing manual work and avoiding oversells.

How SKUPlugs Makes This Integration Effortless

The SKUPlugs service is built for simplicity. Retailers don’t need to write code or manage servers—setup is handled through a guided authentication flow and an intuitive dashboard.

Real-time inventory updates

Sales and returns recorded in Lightspeed adjust stock on your Squarespace store instantly to prevent overselling.

Automated product sync

Product data—title, SKU, description, price, images and variants—are pushed to Squarespace automatically.

Order centralization

Online orders from Squarespace are pushed into Lightspeed so you can fulfill and report from one place.

Pricing & promotions

Price changes and promotions made in Lightspeed are reflected on the website without manual updates.
